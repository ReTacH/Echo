Here is what we've found out about Echo Chamber in Twitter:

Current Findings

According to the research Political Discourse on Social Media: Echo Chambers, Gatekeepers, and the Price of Bipartisanship., political echo chambers exist. In particular, the distribution of production and consumption polarities of users is clearly bi-modal, and the production and consumption polarities are highly correlated.[1]
